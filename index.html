Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Share Expenses Plus is a privacy-first expense splitting app. Your financial data remains private and under your control.

Data We Collect

We collect NO personal data.

  • No account registration required
  • No email address collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Storage

All groups, expenses and settlements are stored locally on your device. We operate no backend servers and cannot access your data.

In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store payment information.

Camera & Photo Access

Camera or photo access is used only for adding payment proof images. All photos remain stored locally on your device.

Biometric Authentication

Biometric authentication is used only for app lock functionality. Biometric data never leaves your device.
